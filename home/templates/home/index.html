About Go-Tennis

Who we are

Go-Tennis provides tennis lessons in the heart of London since 2005. Located in East London, Go-Tennis has been the go to professionals to help all adults with their tennis game. We are particularly popular for providing fun, recreational and yet productive tennis courses for those of all standards. Our coaches speak many languages (English, Italian, Spanish, French, Russian). They are all LTA licensed, insured, DBS checked, and First Aid qualified. Our services can cater to complete beginners all the way up to ATP and WTA ranked players.

Our Ethos

Our aim doesn't stop at teaching tennis skills. We strive to teach life skills such as respect, friendliness, politeness, camaraderie, team bulding, leadership, competitiveness and reliability.
