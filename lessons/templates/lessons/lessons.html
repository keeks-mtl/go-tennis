About Lessons

Go-Tennis provides adult tennis coaching located in Stoke Newington, Borough of Hackney, East London. Adult tennis lessons for all levels.

Go-Tennis is an innovative social enterprise, it was founded in 2012 sharing the Lawn Tennis Association's (LTA) vision to open tennis up to anyone and everyone. With the firm purpose of providing access to high-quality tennis coaching and professional management services into places it has never been before, developing successful people in all aspects of life, using tennis as the vehicle for personal development.

Beginner Lessons

This class is designed to introduce new players to the basics of tennis. Throughout the course, adults will cover groundstrokes, footwork, netplay and serve. Learn techniques, practice drills, and approaches to the game of tennis, by learning tennis fundamentals and basic skills.

Intermediate Lessons

This course is for players with some basic tennis skills who can hit an oncoming ball regularly. Here players will further develop the technical forms of their forehand, backhand, volley and serve. Players will also develop a greater understanding of the net play, footwork, court placement, and basic tactics.

Advanced Lessons

This course is focused on competition. It will be an important physical and tactical part, finding the individual's natural technique and tactics for each player, getting the max of them in a game environment. Players will be invited to join our tennis teams in order to start competing in local tournaments.

Private Lessons

Go-Tennis has a group of professionals who are focused and committed to work on the game of each individual. Each player has a potential, which we develop through sessions that will be suitable for each individual in particular, and we help them form their own style, which will guide them to find solutions to the problems on the court.
